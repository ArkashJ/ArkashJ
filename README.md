### What I'm doing now

**Head of Forward Deployed Engineering at [Benmore Technologies](https://benmore.tech)** — leading FDE practice across SMB AI engagements (SaaS, healthcare, NIL athletics, compliance). Employee #2.

Previously: ML researcher at the **[Kirchhausen Lab](https://kirchhausen.hms.harvard.edu/people/arkash-jain-ms-bs)**, Harvard Medical School / Boston Children's Hospital. First-authored **[SpatialDINO](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2025.02.04.636474)** — the first 3D self-supervised vision transformer for cryo-electron tomography. Beat a prior approach co-led by a Nobel laureate.

### Background

Graduated **Magna Cum Laude** from Boston University (2024) with a combined BA in Mathematics + CS and MS in Computer Science (4-year accelerated). Marvin Freedman Scholar (1/6 in math dept). NSF UROP scholar (1/5 freshmen university-wide).

Internships at **[Battery Ventures](https://www.battery.com)** (2× sourcing + diligence), **[Boston Children's Hospital](https://www.childrenshospital.org/)** (ALS resource discovery, WCAG 2.1 AA), and **ZeroSync** (production Rust + NATS JetStream + Merkle-tree POC).
